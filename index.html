THE COME UP
Born in Staten Island, raised in Queens, New York really set the foundation for how I reason, observe, construct and communicate. New York not only taught me to be street smart, but has also allowed me to immerse myself in cultural, lifestyle, culinary and fashion experiences, which influenced my perspective for consuming and creating content.

In 2005, my mother and I embarked on a new journey by moving to Miami, Florida for a change in scenery. Miami had everything New York had and a little more. My fondest memories were my mom walking me down Miami Beach after school, as well as watching exotic cars drive by as we walked past the Versace Mansion. Though Miami’s known for its luxurious lifestyle, one memory that is priceless to me is experiencing Hurricane Katrina. That’s a memory that really shaped my strength and perseverance.

Now residing in Toronto, my journey continues as I embrace new passions and embark on more adventures. I’m grateful to be able to look back at memories and experiences as lessons I’ve learned and stories I can share. With gratitude I’m able to use my past to help enhance my focus, my diligence, being thorough, as well as innovative, which plays a huge part in my love for videography and graphic design. I first got involved in video by filming and editing “Call of Duty” videos, to later stream online. Soon after, I studied Broadcast Journalism at Sheridan College. During my time there I received the opportunity to intern at the Discovery Channel, which led to my career at Discovery, for the next two years.
